Strategy

In chess the main aim is to capture your opponent's king

This can be achieved through careful calculation and strategy from the very first move

Piece Values

  • Pawn - 1
  • Knight - 3
  • Bishop - 3
  • Rook - 5
  • Queen - 9
  • King - 4

The first move

This is where you launch your attack on the center squares with your opening moves

As the game develops, you also need to consider the defence of your own king
You may castle your king with the rook to keep the king out of danger
